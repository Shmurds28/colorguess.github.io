RGB Color Model

The RGB color model is an additive color model in which red, green, and blue light are added together in various ways to reproduce a broad array of colors. The name of the model comes from the initials of the three additive primary colors, red, green, and blue.

How To Play

Choose a color that matches the rgb commbination shown. You can switch from easy, medium or hard depending on the level you want to play. You can also select the "New Colors" button to get a new rgb commbination and new colors to choose from. If you select a wrong color, it will be removed from the disaplayed colors. Once you select the right color, all the squares will change to the selected color and you will have completed the game. A "Play Again?" button will apear when you complete the game and you can click that to play again. Good Luck!
